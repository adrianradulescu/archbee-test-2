The FISPAN Approval Portal allows for a seamless experience from payment initiation in your ERP to the release of payments. If you are using the Approval Portal, all payments (including positive pay) will be held for approval.

## Using the Approval Portal

![](https://images.archbee.com/MM6nC_bKbIkwCRqiFZqEu/RHpFrLDW0LL083LA53n5g_screen-shot-2022-11-21-at-40543-pm.png?format=webp "Open Payment Approval")

Once you log into after the Approval Portal has been activated, you can find a 'Open Payment Approval' button by hovering over the Payment Approval card in the Control Center.

***

![](https://images.archbee.com/MM6nC_bKbIkwCRqiFZqEu/ETAzkiUxpgj7ul0N7r-7s_10c6d625-ccb8-47e7-a8aa-57f5f247656d1201a.jpeg?format=webp "Process a Payment in your ERP bank plugin")

Process a payment through the Pay Bills page in the bank plugin of your ERP.

*Please note that the example above depicts the Pay Bills page in the Sage Intacct bank plugin. This page may look different depending on which ERP you have installed the bank plugin in.&#x20;*

***

![](https://images.archbee.com/MM6nC_bKbIkwCRqiFZqEu/epSUO_RgM-HW5wEJy5dKJ_image.png?format=webp "View the Pending Payments")

A permissioned user logs into the Payables Approval Portal to view payments sent from the plugin for **Rejection&#xA0;**&#x6F;r **Approval** + **Release.**
